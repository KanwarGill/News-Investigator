System Metaphor
The best way to describe the application is an investigator.
- The investigator will get all the information from the researcher.
-	The investigator will investigate the topic and look for information coming from other topics/people/sources.
-	For the facts to be reliable they need to come directly from the source. The investigator will continue to search for other people until the original source is found.
-	Once all the relationships between articles are found, the investigator will create a report.
-	The report will be published and also archived for future use.
